About us

Pitchtarget is proudly brought to you by Addictive, an Italian start-up born in 2014. Most part of the team was part of a gaming start-up and felt the need of learning more about Facebook Ads and automating the repetitive tasks involved in setting up campaigns and building user audiences. Our journey started there: during an hackathon we hacked with the Facebook Marketing API and in the next days came up with a first prototype of what later would have evolved into Pitchtarget. In March 2014 we have been selected, together with other 9 European start-ups, to be part of the first PMD Facebook Accelarate Program.

In October, 2014, Addictive has been selected as one of the 9 winners of OpenAxel, a competition that finds the best entrepreneurial talents in Europe and awards them with world class acceleration services.

Addictive's team has a deep knowledge of the Facebook Marketing API and is constantly updated on the latest news about the Facebook platform.
